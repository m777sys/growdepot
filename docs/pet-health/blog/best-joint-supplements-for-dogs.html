When Does Your Dog Need Joint Supplements?

Don't wait for obvious limping. Start supplements when you notice:

  • Reluctance to jump on furniture or into cars
  • Stiffness after rest (morning hobbling)
  • Lagging behind on walks they used to lead
  • Difficulty standing from a lying position
  • Behavioral changes (avoiding play, irritability)
Proactive Timing: Large breeds (Labradors, Golden Retrievers, German Shepherds) should start preventive supplements at age 2-3 before problems appear.

Proper Dosage by Weight

Most supplements (including our top picks) follow this dosing:

  • Under 25 lbs: 1 chew daily (initial 4-6 weeks: 2 chews)
  • 25-50 lbs: 2 chews daily (initial: 4 chews)
  • 50-75 lbs: 3 chews daily (initial: 5 chews)
  • 75+ lbs: 4 chews daily (initial: 6 chews)
Important: Always check ingredient concentrations, not just chew count. Some budget brands require 3-4 chews to equal 1 of our recommended products.
