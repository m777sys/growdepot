What You DON'T Need (Yet)

  • CO2 systems — Overkill for first grow. Maybe at grow #5.
  • Expensive nutrients — The FoxFarm trio works. Don't overthink it.
  • Fancy controllers — A $35 WiFi hygrometer does 90% of what you need.
  • Expensive seeds — Start cheap. You'll kill a few plants. Everyone does.

💡 Pro Tips From Trial and Error

  • Start with 2 plants, not 4. Less to manage while you're learning.
  • Spend the extra $30-50 on a decent tent. Cheap tents leak light and frustrate you for months.
  • Get the ventilation kit. Your neighbours will thank you.
  • Photoperiod strains are more forgiving than autos for beginners. More time to catch mistakes.

Timeline: From Setup to Harvest

  • Days 1-5: Order gear, set up tent, germinate seeds
  • Weeks 1-3: Vegetative growth (18 hours light, 6 dark)
  • Week 4: Switch to flower (12/12 light cycle)
  • Weeks 5-9: Flower phase (8-10 weeks depending on strain)
  • Week 10: Harvest, dry, cure
